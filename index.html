ABOUT US

An Oasis in the Heart of Kreševo

Restaurant Regius is not just a place to eat, it’s an experience. Located within Hotel Adriale, along the Kreševčica River with a view of the old monastery, it offers a unique blend of nature, history, and gastronomy. Our chefs create dishes that carry the spirit of Bosnia, enriched with modern and international flavors. With us, you’ll feel the warmth of traditional cuisine as well as the excitement of new culinary discoveries.
